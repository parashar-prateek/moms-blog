Now let's talk about THE LADY OF JUSTICE STATUE, what this statute represents, you will see three major symbols in this statute:

- She is holding WEIGHING SCALE (balance) in her left hand which relates to equality, harmony and fairness in the judicial process and this symbol also signifies that evidences which are produced during the trial will be given full weightage so that judgement can be given after taking all the evidence into account!

- The SWORD symbolises the power of law and enforcement of the final judgment/ruling deliver by the courts after pursuing all the evidence!

- THE BLIND FOLD represents impartial and unbiased justice means that how powerful, wealthy individuals one can be, justice system being blind to all such kind of influences!
