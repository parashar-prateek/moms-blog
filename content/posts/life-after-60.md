Every other day we receive posts and videos regarding life after 60+ years of age. A lot of these posts, portray a negative approach towards life. One should not have hard feelings towards people who have attained the age of 60+.

Usually people who write such posts and videos have the opinion that after attaining the age of 60 or above-families and society starts to distance itself from them, which is not true!

People who have worked hard all their lives, lived frugally and had some basics investments are able to live gracefully and with respect, enjoying the fruits of their labour in this phase of life!

Sometimes best phase of life begins after 60! Some of the best opportunities in life people get after 60! This phase of life is fulfilling and rewarding which gives you immense satisfaction that the sacrifices, compromises and hardships you put in your early years of life now pays off like dividends! So people who are 60+ have every right to choose the way they want to live their life! They still have lots to offer to their families and to the society!

Iss sansar mai anna or iss sansar sey jaana sab prabhu k haath mein hai! No body has the power to eliminate other person! Enjoy the best phase of life which only begins after 60 and no need to pay any heed to posts, videos which says otherwise!

Life is precious and you get only one life do whatever makes you happy and brings smile to your face, follow your passion.

We are living in the era of technology, be careful and be safe from the fraudsters who are very active and are trying to steal hard earned money from the people! Last but not least be thankful and grateful for everything that we all have in our life!

Stay strong and stay safe and be proud of yourself and your accomplishments!!
