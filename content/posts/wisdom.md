In this wold of fools every one considers themselves as wise person, most of the people never know how to act or how to react when they see something that might be wrong and that might effect their own lives.

Where people don't have their own perception /opinion or awareness in such cases they are unable to act or react?

Wisdom can give you a sense for how to act and react in situations where you have no control over them, wisdom introduce you to a different perspective which allows you to feel more confident about yourself and also gives you opportunities to make your life more fulfilling by taking right decisions at a right time.

Wisdom also allows you to become a better version of yourself by taking positive steps toward achieving your goals/dreams. Always keep a positive outlook in your mind so that good vibes can be created around your surroundings.

Wisdom has a wider range of qualities that make you stronger, healthier, happy, humble, more patient, make you think positive about yourself.

Now how to achieve wisdom? Any one who is ready to learn from his past mistakes, experiences and failures and wants to improve their own lives will have to take certain steps I.e. by cultivating self awareness, self confidence, by stepping out of your comfort zones, always surround yourself with people who are genuinely interested in your growth, don't compare yourself with others, having positive attitude towards self development, these small steps can help you to achieve and grow as a positive person in the long run.

As we age and grow older we become more self sufficient and also become emotionally stable, if we maintain and take precautions to keep ourselves healthy then we can retain our own wisdom and can pass on to others who are willing to take the risk of becoming a better person but once your physical decline starts then gradually your wisdom will also be declining, so take care of your own health and stay wise and healthy till the end.
