What is music, for me music is SKOON, music is a language and a mode of communication. Though I don't no the technical term for music, I think it's the most popular language to communicate your emotions and feelings and everyone will understands what you're trying to convey!

Music has the power and ability to bring joy and comfort in the lives of the people specially for those who are affected by the loss of their loved ones. Whenever you feel low in life listen to music of your choice or you can play any musical instrument and while listening music, if you want to cry, cry as much as you can and after that you will feel better and relaxed because music always touches your soul and your unspoken feelings and gives you chance to recover from your past.

Music is good for stress relief, music can heal and help you to recover from stress, depression and anxiety. Music helps in regulating your mood swings in the process of recovery, it also improves our physical health and our ability to cope with the changes that occur during the recovery process. Music provides entertainment and it improves your creativity.

The most important aspect of music is that IT HELPS THE BRAIN TO DEVELOP, it activates our senses. It creates a deeper emotional connection. It provides a sense of equality between humans!

These days everyone is under pressure, specially the young generation and that pressure to become a successful person in life sometimes affects their mental health and that is the reason why some big big companies organising globally classical music concerts for positive mental health of their employees, these concerts helps people to experience a transformative change in their lives.

Music has no age limit, at any stage of your life you can learn music, if you people can spare some time from your routine, then go for it. Though I never had any music training in my entire life but I am passionate about music since my childhood and because of that passion, I am quite proud of myself that I am not a bad SINGER.

IF YOU REALLY WANT TO HEAL YOUR INVISIBLE WOUNDS THEN GO FOR MUSIC.
