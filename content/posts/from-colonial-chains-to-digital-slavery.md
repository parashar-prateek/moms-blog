The British East India Company entered India via Calcutta with the intension to secure exclusive trading rights in the Indian Ocean, breaking the Portuguese and Dutch monopolies! This trading company later ruled large part of India from 1757 to 1857 transforming itself from a trading body into a colonial administrative after battle of Plassey in 1757 fought between East India Company led by Lord Clive and last independent Nawab of Bengal Saraju-ud-Daulah!

East India Company won this battle due to the betrayal of Mir Jaffer who was commander of Nawab's army! Once the Nawab lost the battle of Plassey the rise of East India Company began and Company took over the full political and military control in Bengal and doors were opened for colonial rule in India.

The East India Company ruled India from 1757 to 1857 and after this British Crown rule began and they ruled in India from 1858 to 14.8.1947. The total duration of British rule was nearly 200 years (1757 to 14.8.1947).

During these two hundred years of British rule lakhs of Indians transported as labourers to other British Colonies across the globe against their wishes, this was FORCED transportation of labourers resulting lakhs of families separated from their loved ones, innocent people of India faced profound hardship and sufferings. Britishers exploited Indian economic! Indian subcontinent which was global economic hub collapsed and turned into one of the world's poorest nation! Indian industries were downgraded as machine made goods were promoted! Britishers literally looted India and left behind scars of their atrocities and destruction in each and every field!

We have not yet recovered from their shadow as India's judiciary and education system largely influenced by British colonial policies! Government is trying to decolonize these systems by introducing certain reforms in criminal laws and in national educational policy (NEP) let's see how impact full these reforms will be?

NO INDIAN HAD EVER OPTED FOR THIS 200 YEARS OF BRITISH RULE! The Britisher imposed system of FORCED LABOUR on Indians. Slavery in British India was abolished by the INDIAN SLAVERY ACT 1843 but even after the abolition of Slavery act there was hardly any improvement in the condition of Indians! After decades of sufferings, hardship India finally gained independence from British Rule on 15.8.47!!
