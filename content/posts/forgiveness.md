There are different types of people in this world and they all have different personalities and every person has different kinds of problems and challenges! To overcome these problems and challenges some people believes in short cuts and want to achieve their dreams by taking rough, rigorous, tough and troublesome steps towards achieving their goals without realising the repercussions of their actions, that their actions may or might affect someone's life and livelihood you will find such people in your own family, in your work place, in your community in sports and in general you will find them in different places of work.

Sometimes it's very easy to give advice to the people to please forgive those who spoiled someone's career, hurts someone's feelings, sentiments, emotions, betrayals at any stage of their life, that time the advisors don't realise that how impactful was/is the hurt that it spoiled/spoil someone's life for ever and pain given by the person/person's will never be forgotten and FORGIVE

Though it's very very hard to pardon someone who played vital role in making someone's life miserable but unforgiveness keeps miseries and pain of your life alive and reminding you of the sufferings which you have experienced and won't allow you to move on in life!

WHEREAS

FORGIVENESS is a choice, many people apologies for their sins and mistakes but they are not meant to be taken seriously by the people who were suffered and are suffering, there is a saying when some one we TRUSTED BETRAYS us, it can be hard to forgive them and REBUILD that trust. FORGIVENESS doesn't mean forgetting the sufferings and harm done to the individuals BUT if someone who has a very very big heart and has desire to reconcile with the circumstances and wants to forgive people who once given him or her the pain, by doing so:

--He or she is releasing the pain and the feeling of RESENTMENT OR ANGER

--When you forgive, YOU GIVE A CHANCE TO YOURSELF TO HEEL YOUR WOUNDS

--FORGIVENESS gives us opportunity to free ourselves from holding on the things which causes us pain and anxieties!

--It allows us to repair the emotional damage caused by the actions of our enemies

FORGIVENESS allows us to take a step forward AND MOVE ON IN LIFE!

Why it's very very hard to forget and forgive people, REASON is in real life we quickly forget good times and keep bitter memories in our hearts and minds forever, which prevents us to forget and forgive!

As per my opinion Let the person who had undergone the hardest time in life DECIDES whether the people who have made his or her life miserable deserves FORGIVENESS OR NOT! ONLY AFFECTED PERSON KNOWS HOW HARD WAS THE HURT!
