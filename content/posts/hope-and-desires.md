Never allow hope and desires to die down! Hope symbolises LIGHT and DESIRES keeps us going forward and give us energy to grow stronger and more resilient in our lives!

Our DESIRES sets the stage for our goals to achieve, few desires we share with others where we don't mind expressing our thoughts, feelings and ideas and where we are ready to take help and guidance from others! The other types of desires are HIDDEN DESIRES and everyone has some sort of hidden desires which won't allow us to express ourselves or share our feelings, thoughts — this situation leads to discomfort, anger and frustration and then fight between you and your mind starts which causes tremendous stress, pain and exhaustion! To avoid stress and anger in life, it's always better to share, to express your thoughts, feelings or emotions with someone who is willing to listen to your concerns about your situation!

So one thing is very clear that whether you are having EXPRESSED DESIRES OR HIDDEN DESIRES, desires keep you moving forward motivates you to achieve your goals and fulfill your dreams and gives you opportunities to grow and compete in every aspect of life!

Imagine If there is no desire left in our lives then gradually we lose the urge to live and life becomes dull, monotonous, aimless and meaningless, so always keep HOPE and DESIRES alive!

Sometimes too much desires, too much expectations affects our peace of mind that's true but if some desires brings growth, happiness, joy, fullness and internal satisfaction then we must keep such desires alive forever! Always HOPE that one day all DESIRES will come TRUE!! Maintain POSTIVITY which increases the chance to WIN! Take care and stay strong!!
