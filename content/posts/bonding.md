There are different types of bonding in the world and the most precious bond is between Mother and her children, this bonding starts even when the child is in her mother's womb. Every mother wants best of everything for her children and once the child is born, both parents try their best to give good quality life and good family values to their children.

Once the children start growing then bonding also start expanding I.e bonding with siblings, this bond between them stays strong till they all are fully grown but bond between parents and children will grow stronger and stronger as time passes by? Gradually bond between mother and daughter turns into best friends. As time goes by family becomes very important for each and every member of the family.

The bond between humans and the animals, you can name this bonding as HEALING BONDING really this bonding teaches you love, loyalty and trust and heals your old and fresh wounds given by your own people or by the society, animals have the power to change any dimension of your life. Animals can heal your not visible pain and suffering!

The bonding where we are not having any blood relation but our wisdom gives us the opportunity to choose our "FRIENDS". Where two mind-sets share a common vision, interest, purpose, passion and where their IQ level matches up then they can become good friends for the rest of their life, but it's not necessary that everyone is lucky enough to get good people in their life.

It's not important how many friends you have, important is even, IF, one intelligent, sincere, loyal, committed, honest and genuine person enters your life and becomes your best friend and if He OR She has the ability to turn around your life I.e. more than enough!

Most of us are living last leg of our lives and everyone is living best version of themselves by celebrating our lives with our extended family and friends. Take care of each other and if someone needs any support, help don't hesitate to reach out to your colleagues and friends, life is too hard and short so please grow the seeds of happiness and peace everywhere, Stay Strong
